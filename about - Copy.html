About As

Madarsa Jamiya Safiya Lilbanat is a school for girls up to 10th grade, providing a combination of Modern and Islamic Education. The school was established in 2008 by the Shiksha Sadbhawna Welfare Jamiya Safiya Lilbanat Samiti, located in Mohalla Ahmadi Tola, Kannauj, Uttar Pradesh, India. The school provides free education to underprivileged and orphan girls. The curriculum includes subjects like Quran, Hadith, Fiqh,

Arabic, English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies and General Knowledge. In addition to these subjects,

students are also given training in computer skills.The aim of the school is to provide quality education to underprivileged girls and help them become self-confident and independent individuals.

The school has been successful in achieving its goals and has produced many confident and independent women who are contributing positively to society.

There are many madaris in India which only provide Islamic education to students. However, Madarsa Jamiya Safiya Lilbanat in Haji Ganj, Kannauj, Uttar Pradesh, India is unique in that it provides a combination of modern and Islamic education to its students up to the 10th grade.

The school was established in 2008 by Maulana Mohd Shamoon Sahab Mazahiri for girls seeing huge gap in girl’s education. The school follows the Uttar Pradesh Madarsa Board of Education curriculum for its modern education classes, and Darul Uloom Deoband for its Islamic Education system.

The school has been very successful in providing its students with an education that prepares them for both the modern world and their religious duties. The majority of the school’s graduates go on to pursue higher studies at universities.

The school is affiliated with the Board of Madarsa Education, Lucknow, and offers education up to 10th standard. The school has a unique combination of modern and Islamic education, which makes it an ideal choice for Muslim girls.

The school offers a wide range of subjects, including English, Hindi, Urdu, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Islamic Studies. In addition to these academic subjects, the school also offers extracurricular activities such as sports and cultural events. The school has a strong emphasis on providing quality education to its students. The teachers are highly qualified and experienced in their respective fields.

They use innovative teaching methods to ensure that the students understand the concepts taught in class. The school has a well-stocked library with a wide variety of books on different subjects. The library also has a separate section for Islamic literature. The school also has a computer lab with internet connection so that the students can access online resources. The school provides an open and inclusive environment where every student is given equal opportunity to succeed. It follows a zero-tolerance policy.

Shiksha Sadbhawna Welfare Jamiya Safiya Lilbanat Samiti is a non-profit organization that manages Madarsa Jamiya Safiya Lilbanat, a school for girls up to 10th with combination of modern and Islamic education. The organization’s mission is to provide an excellent education to Muslim girls so that they can become productive citizens of India. The organization strives to create an environment where girls can learn and grow in an Islamic atmosphere.

The organization has a Board of Trustees which governs the operations of the school. The Board consists of parents, educators, and community leaders. The Board provides guidance and oversight to ensure that the school is providing a quality education to its students.

The organization raises funds through donations from individuals, businesses, and foundations. These funds are used to support the operation of the school and provide scholarships for deserving students.
